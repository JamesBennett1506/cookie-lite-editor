Privacy Policy for Cookie Lite Editor

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Data Collection
Cookie Lite Editor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All cookie data is accessed locally within your browser and is never sent to any server.

What We Access
The extension reads and modifies cookies on the websites you visit strictly for the purpose of managing them as per your commands. This includes:
- Cookie names and values
- Cookie domains, paths, and expiration dates
- Security flags (Secure, HttpOnly, SameSite)

Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or external services are used.
